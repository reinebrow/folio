About meGet to know more

I'm Reine (Rain), a web and graphic designer from Mindoro, Philippines. My design journey began in high school with a passion for arts and crafts. Now, I specialize in static websites using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and a bit of PHP and MySQL for more dynamic projects. I also create materials for digital and printing. In my free time, I enjoy mock-painting and embracing my "plantito" side during the days. Music from Ariana Grande, BTS, Taylor Swift, Blackpink, and Little Mix keeps me company while I work. I love singing and dancing, especially to K-pop tunes.

That's a snapshot of my life. I hope you've gotten to know me a bit better! I'd love to connect, so feel free to reach out through my social media accounts or drop me an email. Looking forward to hearing from you!
